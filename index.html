How It Works

1. Start a collection with a purpose and a target amount for each person

This is a new RONDAFEL entity, unique to your group, secure and 100% guaranteed.

2. Invite your friends to join the group

Add close friends from your contacts.

3. Group members decide when and how to contribute

Link to Paylpal/Venmo and credit/debit card. Contributions are flexible and gradual.

4. Stay engaged, communicate, and have fun collecting money gradually

Everyone gets notifications of all group activity such as group expenses, new contributions, new joiners, dropped members and group decisions.
