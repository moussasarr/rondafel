About Us

Our vision is to create a new financial ecosystem composed of family-like financial units – rondafel. Each rondafel is an entity interacting with global finance and consumer markets with greater negotiating power.

Our values are informed and inspired by our roots in traditional Africa. The word rondafel is Afrikaans for the little clay and thatch huts that dot the African countryside.

Our people are experts and visionaries. We have been at the forefront of financial innovation for the last decade including structured global finance, microfinance, crypto-currencies and online payments.
