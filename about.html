About Us

We are all about having meaningful relationships, sharing life experiences and growing together. These are the ingredients for happiness. But to have these things you need trust, friends and a little money all in the same pot. That’s why we built RONDAFEL - to help you organize better and trust that you can put your money where your heart is; with friends and family.

With RONDAFEL you will have more shared experiences because it’s easier to collect money for group plans. You will save more for goals shared with friends and you may even invest more because making money together feels awesome.

Our mission is to create a financial network made of many rondafel units. Each one interacting with retailers and markets with greater negotiating power.

Because together we are better; we are stronger.

RONDAFEL is connected money.


Team


Clynton Kakai, CEO & Co-Founder

Clynton has worked in Structured Finance at some really large New York Investment banks for many years. He currently does Strategy and architecture for trading and risk management systems.
Despite Wall Street distractions, Clynton co-founded two ventures in Nairobi-Campus Diary Media and Bona Wireless. He enjoys science fiction, soccer and the tenor saxaphone.

Michael Wangia, CTO & Co-Founder

Born Canadian and raised keynan, Michael moved to Philadelphia in 2006 to study Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania and later mastered in Information Systems at Drexel University. He is an accomplished developer with 7 years of experience and currently works with Dell Boomi. Michael founded and continues work on Speckles, a location based retail specials application.
In a previous life, Michael was a professional deejay and played rugby every weekend. These days he enjoys soccer and Java.
